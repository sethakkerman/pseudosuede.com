SF WEEKLY PSA

→

This project was completed for SF Weekly. The goal of the project was to create a companion piece to go along with a cover story they published regarding stray cats in San Francisco. I chose to take a humorous approach to the commercial. A draft version was featured in IdN Magazine, Volume 17, Number 5, 2010.

Classification - Motion Graphics, Commercial
Client - SF Weekly
Typeface - Archer
Production - Asking friends for pet photographs -> Writing / Recording Song -> After Effects

Featured - IdN Magazine, Volume 17, Number 5, 2010
