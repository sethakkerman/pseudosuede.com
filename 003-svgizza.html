# SVG, Processing, Animation, Pizza

Description

If there is one thing you need to know about me, it’s that I love pizza. Real pizza. Crappy microwave pizza. Heck, even those fruit pizzas people make in the summer! When learning new technologies, I find it most productive if I can incorporate something that I know will keep my interest up. Thus, the SVGIZZA was born. I like to consider myself the best pizza coder on either side of the Mississippi.

Follow along with my digital pizza cooking exercises at pseudosuede.com/svgizza.
