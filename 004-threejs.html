# three.js, 3D, Modeling

Description

I took a three.js workshop at Telfair Museums from Vince Mckelvie. It was amazing. I have a background modeling in SketchUp, AutoCAD, and Revit so I was excited to see the possibilities of extending that knowledge to the browser.

During the workshop I built this weird metal, geometric, octopus. Have patience, it takes a moment to load.
