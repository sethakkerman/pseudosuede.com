SAVANNAH SECESSION

→

This campaign consisted of the postcard advertisements and gallery catalog for the graphic design department’s 2009 senior show. The juried work consisted of both graduate and undergraduate work. I was selected to be on the team to produce these elements.

Classification - Exhibition Catalog, Exhibition Design
Client - SCAD Graphic Design Department
Typefaces - ITC Officina Sans and ITC Officina Serif
Production - Saddle-stitched catalog, Postcard, Exhibition, 3D Glasses
Paper - Neenah Environmental Bright White
Paper Size - 8” x 10” (catalog), 5” x 7” (postcard)

Featured - NOTCOT

Awards / Recognition - This campaign was awarded a Gold Medal at the 2009 UCDA Design Competition.
